About

Hello! My name is Raiya. I'm a second-year computer science student, currently studying at Simon Fraser University in Burnaby, British Columbia. My coding journey began only a short time ago, when I decided to self-learn how to build websites using HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and I soon realized that I had found my calling.

I'm passionate about filmmaking, graphic design, web development and, on the other end of the spectrum, about public health and social sustainability. Ultimately, I hope to navigate into a career that lies somewhere at the intersection of technology and health.
